FAQ

+

Is my personal information safe?

We care about your privacy. You can read our Privacy Policy for more. The Privacy Policy is part of our Terms of Service.

+

How do I close my account?

We hate to see you go, but if you insist, see how to close your account.

+

What is online status?

The Online Status is indicated by a small green circle next to the username, which lets you know that the user is currently logged in to Fiverr's website or mobile application. For more information on defining your online status, see Managing Your Account.

+

How long does it take for me to receive revenues from an order?

Buyers pay Fiverr for orders in advance. Once the order is created and started, Sellers must fulfill their orders as described in their Gig description and must deliver completed files and/or proof of work using the Deliver Your Order button (located on the Order page). Once work is delivered, the Buyer has three days to respond and post a review (or 14 days for gigs with shipping).

+

Are there any fees when withdrawing revenue?

There are no fees for withdrawing through PayPal. Withdrawing by Bank Transfer or Direct deposit also incur fees, see Withdrawing Funds.
